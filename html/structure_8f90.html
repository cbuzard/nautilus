module  structure
 

Module that contains all the routines linked to the physical structure (cloud and such) and its properties.


Functions/Subroutines

subroutine structure::init_structure_evolution ()
 Read time evolution for the physical structure in structure_evolution.dat to initialize arrays. Thus, interpolation of physical structure properties throughout the simulation be possible.
structure_evolution.dat will have a two lines header. Then columns will be as follow :
Time (Myr) ; Number density (part/cm3) ; Temperature (K) ; Av (mag)
